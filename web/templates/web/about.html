About Jeopractice

This is Jeopractice! It's is the best way to sharpen your Jeopardy! skills. We have over 200,000 clues from over 3,000 shows over the last 30 years. Every time you arrive to Jeopractice, a random game begins. Use the arrow keys to navigate to different questions and categories, and the spacebar to reveal the answers.

Jeopractice was made by Brendan Sudol in the hopes of one day becoming a worthy Jeopardy! adversary for his wife. This website is not affiliated with, sponsored by, or operated by Jeopardy Productions, Inc.
